Chicken Dinner

Image of Chicken Recipe, credit: Lillie
The purpose of this recipe is to provide the framework for a high protein dinner recipe which also has vitamins
Ingredients
  • 10-20g of Pre-cooked Chicken Protein
  • 1 cup Fajita veggies
  • 2 Tablespoon of olive oil
  • 1 sheet Lavash bread
  • 1 cup of plain yogurt
  • 1 cup of oat milk
Steps
  1. Pour 1 tablespoon of olive oil on a medium heated skillet
  2. Cook the pre-cooked chicken protein per package instructions on skillet. Season as required
  3. Eat chicken, and then add a tablespoon of olive oil and fajita veggies. Season as required
  4. Cook veggies thoroughly until browned. In the midst of cooking, tear up a piece of lavash into 4 quadrants and place them in groups of 2 into a toaster on low setting for 10-15 seconds
  5. Eat veggies with the lavash bread.
  6. Eat a few spoons of the plain yogurt
  7. Finish the meal with 1 cup of oat milk
