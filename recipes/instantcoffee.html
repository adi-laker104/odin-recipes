Instant Coffee Recipe

Image of Instant Coffee Recipe, credit: Food Republic
The purpose of this recipe is to provide a quick and cheap option to make a cup of coffee
Ingredients
  • 1 teaspoon of instant coffee mix
  • 3/4 cup hot water
  • 1 packet brown sugar
  • 1/4 cup 2% milk
Steps
  1. First boil water in a kettle
  2. Open the brown sugar packet. Pour the brown sugar and instant coffee into an empty cup
  3. Once water is boiled, pour the hot water into the cup
  4. At this point, mix the contents with a spoon
  5. Last, add the milk to the cup and mix again with a spoon. It is ready!
